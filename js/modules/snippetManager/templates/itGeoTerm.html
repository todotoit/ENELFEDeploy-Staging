Geothermal power was first harnessed in Italy over a century ago, at the Larderello plant that is now part of a huge 34-plant complex between Pisa, Siena and Grosseto.

With a total capacity of almost 6 GWh, the complex covers the energy needs of 2 million families, or 27% of Tuscany.
